AWH Engineering College is a private self-financing institution located in Kuttikkattoor, Kozhikode, Kerala. The college was established in 2001 and is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). It is approved by AICTE and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Departments:
- Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
- Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- Mechanical Engineering (ME)
- Civil Engineering (CE)
- Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS)

Facilities:
- Well-equipped labs and workshops
- Central Library with digital resources
- Hostel facilities for boys and girls
- Sports and recreation facilities
- Cafeteria and canteen
- Transportation services

Clubs and Activities:
- IEEE Student Branch
- IEDC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell)
- NSS (National Service Scheme)
- MuLearn Community
- Technical and cultural fests
